Tiramisu

Description

There's no better way to end a meal than with a classic tiramisu.

Ingredients

  • Egg yolks
  • White sugar
  • Whole milk
  • Heavy cream
  • Vanilla extract
  • Mascarpone cheese
  • Coffee
  • Rum or Kahl√∫a
  • Ladyfingers
  • Cocoa powder

Steps

  1. Cook the egg yolks, sugar, and milk until slightly thickened.
  2. Let cool slightly, then chill in the fridge for about an hour.
  3. When the filling has fully chilled, mix in mascarpone cheese.
  4. In a separate bowl, beat heavy cream with vanilla extract until stiff peaks form.
  5. Combine coffee and rum in a small bowl.
  6. Pour coffee and rum mixture over ladyfingers that have been split in half lengthwise.
  7. Line the bottom of a baking dish with soaked ladyfingers.
  8. Spread half of the mascarpone mixture over the ladyfingers, then half of the whipped cream over that.
  9. Repeat the above once more.
  10. Finally, dust the tiramisu with cocoa powder.
