Bacon-Wrapped Scallops

Description

Bacon-wrapped scallops is a simple, yet delicious appetizer which combines land with sea.

Ingredients

  • 6 slices bacon
  • 12 sea scallops, rinsed and drained
  • 3 tablespoons fresh lemon juice

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Cut bacon slices in half and wrap each half around a scallop, using a toothpick to secure in place.
  3. Drizzle lemon juice over scallops, placing them on a cookie sheet afterwards.
  4. Bake in a preheated oven for 15 to 20 minutes or until bacon is cooked. Serve warm.
