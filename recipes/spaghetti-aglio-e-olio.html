Spaghetti Aglio e Olio

Description

Every good dish starts with garlic and olive oil. Spaghetti aglio e olio makes these two ingredients the star of the show.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic
  • Crushed red pepper flakes
  • Black pepper
  • Fresh parsley
  • Parmigiano-Reggiano cheese

Steps

  1. Boil the spaghetti.
  2. Toast the garlic in olive oil.
  3. Stir the seasonings into the pasta after draining.
  4. Stir in the garlic and oil.
  5. Toss with cheese.
